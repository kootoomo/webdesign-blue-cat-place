Oh! I can do lots of things!

I can sleep, first of all

I can drink milk,

I can say "purr-purr",

And listen what you tell me,

I can bring you some wildfowl

But foil ball is more preferred.


What I want for my services:

1) warm place to sleep;

2) fresh milk to drink;

3) love me forever.

If you don't mind this would be fine and sufficient.
